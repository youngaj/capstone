Setup Guide

Welcome Boys and Girls Club facilitators to the PowerU Tech Mission Powered by Learning Blade!

This two-part modular experience will help students to immerse into the world of STEM and to create clear pathways into STEM careers! Here are a few instructions for you as facilitators of this experience.

Context: After the STEM-based Escape Room, students can participate in a web-based assignment that aligns with their interests. To enable this opportunity, you will leverage a website called Learning Blade to enroll students quickly and set up activities for students to complete. To get started,

  1. Login to your laptop or desktop
  2. If you are using a laptop, make sure it is having enough power and plugged in
  3. Open your web browser (Chrome or Microsoft Edge)
  4. Type or copy the URL of the learning portal into the web browser.
  5. Save the link to the learning portal.
  6. Hit return and you should enter in the escape learning blade.
  7. Begin your adventure of learning.
