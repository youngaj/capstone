Facilitator Guide

Welcome Boys and Girls Club facilitators to the PowerU Tech Mission Powered by Learning Blade!

This two-part modular experience will help students to immerse into the world of STEM and to create clear pathways into STEM careers! Here are a few instructions for you as facilitators of this experience.

Context: After the STEM-based Escape Room, students can participate in a web-based assignment that aligns with their interests. To enable this opportunity, you will leverage a website called Learning Blade to enroll students quickly and set up activities for students to complete. To get started,

  1. Navigate to web.learningblade.com and login as a teacher:
    • Username - should be your email address
    • Default password - BoysnGirlsClubPower54!
  2. Enroll Students in Learning Blade
  3. Create classes to group students with similar interests, then add assignments
    1. Create a Class
    2. Create teams
    3. Add students to the class
    4. Add students to a team
    5. Add assignments to the class
      • Assignments can be a full mission or specific career path
    6. Limit student view to only see assignments

Helpful hints:
For Teachers

  1. The Tech Escape experience should take between 30 and 45 minutes to complete
  2. The Tech Mission should take approximately 40 minutes to complete. You must set a timer outside of Tech Mission Powered by Learning Blade at the beginning of the experience to count down 40 minutes.
  3. If you accidentally add the wrong mission or Tools and Teammates see Condensed Teach Guide - Step 5
    1. Here’s how the Leader Board works
    2. Log in with your teacher account
    3. Click on Dashboard on the top button menu
    4. Under Leader board, select the appropriate class and mission. (You can only show a leader board for a class, and the mission must be assigned to the class to select it.)
    5. The leader board will display a list of the mission scores for each student in the class.
  4. Only the first response is included in the Student's score even though they will receive a second attempt at the questions.
  5. There are three Missions to choose from, which will be provided from the survey:
    1. Hack Attack
    2. Robot Rescue
    3. Haiti Orphanage
  6. The team with the highest number of points wins.

Helpful hints to provide students

  1. The Tech Mission should take approximately 40 minutes to complete. If you sign out or exit learning blade, when you log back in, press the Green button at the top that says “MISSION GUIDE”
