About PowerU

Exposing our youth to the power and joy of STEM

Welcome to the PowerU STEM experience. It includes hand crafted experiences to both introduce and excite youth about STEM and careers in STEM.

PowerU is an online interactive experience designed for young people to learn about STEM. PowerU is divided into 3 main sections (past, present and future). Each section provides a unique experience. PowerU is the capstone project for Power54, ITSMF Management Academy class of 2023. It is our hope this this project will inspire you pursue a career in STEM.

Past

Tech Escapes - Explore historical inventors and their inventions in the interactive escape room.

Present

Tech Missions - Dive into online training via Learning Blade

Future

PowerU Summary - Continue your learning journey with information on STEM and STEM careers.

ITSMF and Power54

Information Technology Senior Management Forum (ITSMF) was formed in 1996. ITSMF has positioned African American professionals for leadership roles in Information Technology. For over 25 years ITSMF has remained the premier national organization dedicated exclusively to cultivating executive talent amongst African American professionals.

ITSMF is also committed to community outreach programs such as scholarships for African American youths. African American collegiate students are also provided with access to ITSMF Symposia to network with industry leaders from Fortune 500 companies and successful entrepreneurs in STEM-related fields. Students can get mentoring, increased exposure to internship opportunities and attendance to professional conferences and leadership development programs.
