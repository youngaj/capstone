About PowerU

Exposing our youth to the power and joy of STEM

Welcome to the World’s Leading STEM experience. It includes hand crafted experiences to both introduce and excite youth about STEM and careers in STEM.

Information Technology Senior Management Forum (ITSMF) was formed in 1996. ITSMF has positioned black professionals for leadership roles in Information Technology. For over 25 years ITSMF has remained the premier national organization dedicated exclusively to cultivating executive talent amongst African American professionals.

ITSMF is also committed to community outreach programs such as scholarships for black youths. Black collegiate students are also provided with access to ITSMF Symposia to network with industry leaders from Fortune 500 companies and successful entrepreneurs in STEM-related fields. Students can get mentoring, increased exposure to internship opportunities and attendance to professional conferences and leadership development programs.
